857.

Mandara Giridhara Madana Manohara
Meera Ke Prabhu Giridhara Naagara
Giridhari Giridhari Govardhanodhara Giridhari (2)
Madhava Madhusudhana
Murali Gaana Vilola
 

Lord Krishna who lifted the Govardhana mountain which was earlier known as Mandara mountains to save the people from the wrath of Indra, the Lord of Meera, slayer of demon Madhu, loves playing the flute.
