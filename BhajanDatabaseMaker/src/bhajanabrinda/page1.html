Brindamrutam Online

Brindamrutam Online has both old and new Bhajans which were composed and sung by devotees of Bhagawan in Brindavan, Prashanthi Nilayam and other centers. A comprehensive compilation of Bhajans was needed for a devotee aspiring to learn and sing Sai Bhajans. This online Bhajanavali has attempted to be a one stop web reference for any aspiring bhajan singer, giving the complete lyrics, meaning, audio reference and other external references pertaining to the bhajan. The audio references and other reference would be added on an ongoing basis. This will serve as a ready reckoner for all singers to learn and sing with more confidence and devotion.

(Disclaimer:  The Reference audio is only meant to provide some reference for the tune of the bhajan and may not exactly match the lyrics provided)
